# What is Nusuk?
Nusuk (nusuk.sa) نسك is the official digital platform of Saudi Arabia, operated by the Ministry of Hajj and Umrah. It is the authoritative gateway for Muslims worldwide to plan, book, and manage Hajj and Umrah online — replacing external travel agents.

**Hajj (الحج):** The major pilgrimage to Makkah during Dhul-Hijjah. Fifth pillar of Islam; obligatory once in a lifetime upon every adult Muslim physically and financially able. Rites span Makkah, Mina, Arafat, Muzdalifah, and Jamarat.

**Umrah (العمرة):** The minor pilgrimage to Makkah, performable any time of year. Highly recommended but not obligatory. Consists of ihram, tawaf around the Kaaba, sa'i between Safa and Marwah, and shaving or trimming the hair.

## Core Services
eVisa issuance; Hajj and Umrah permits; flights to/from Saudi Arabia; hotels in Makkah and Madinah (3- to 5-star); intercity transport between Makkah, Madinah, and Mashair; catering; guided spiritual and logistical support; interactive holy-site maps; health services; multilingual digital guide; gifting Umrah packages; customizable packages; visa-free packages for GCC citizens, residents, and valid visa holders.
